Dairy diet for weight loss

Milk is a well-known and beloved product. For fans of this product, in order to normalize weight, a dairy diet for weight loss has been invented. Milk contains various minerals that support the condition of bone tissues and improve the condition of skin cells covers'. With the help of a dairy diet for weight loss, you can get rid of three to five kilograms of excess weight in 1-2 weeks.

Types of dairy diet for weight loss

1. to begin with, I recommend trying fasting milk days. The fat content of milk should not exceed 4 percent. According to this meal plan, you need to drink a glass of milk every one and a half to two hours. The total amount of milk should not exceed 1.5 liters. In addition to milk, you can drink water without gas. The consumption of other products is prohibited for three days. During the period of compliance with this type of dairy diet for weight loss user reviews note the appearance of headaches, irritability and General fatigue. The break after milk days should be at least one and a half months.

2. If you are having difficulty using the first diet option, try reducing it duration of up to one day. Such unloading can be performed on a weekly basis. If the body does not tolerate milk well, then for a one-day dairy diet for weight loss, it is permissible to replace it with kefir, ryazhenka or natural yogurt.

3. as an alternative to using a dairy diet for weight loss, it is allowed to add fresh fruit to the diet. In this case, the duration of the diet can be 5 days. You can eat fruit in its original form, you can add it to milk and make smoothies. Fruits should be sweet and sour fruits can cause undesirable reactions from the gastrointestinal tract. When using such a dairy diet for weight loss, the meal time is regulated, you need to eat 4 times a day, the interval is 3 hours. Eating after six o'clock in the evening is not recommended. The limit is imposed by the number of users you should not eat more than four bananas a day.

4. Express diet with the use of dairy products includes drinking green tea with milk. Observing this food regime will allow you to fasten your favorite jeans at the right moment. It is difficult to withstand 2 days of such nutrition, but you will be satisfied with the resulting effect. Preparing a drink is quite easy. Brew green tea with half the desired volume of water, fill the remaining place with warmed milk. Use this tea throughout the day if you experience a strong feeling before going to bed please drink a glass of kefir. In the evening, you do not need to drink tea, you may have difficulty sleeping.

5. the Weekly option is the most sparing option of the milk diet for weight loss. Reviews of people who have experienced this method of getting rid of if you are overweight, there is a slight steady decrease in body weight. In a day, you can consume 400 grams of cottage cheese, in which you can add a small amount of vegetables, fruits and berries. From drinks, water without gas, tea with rosehip and a glass of any fermented milk product are allowed. Sweeten tea and cottage cheese can be small the amount of honey.

The appearance of low-fat dairy products in stores and their wide range increases the effectiveness of the dairy diet for weight loss. Reviews of people who lose weight in this type of diet, they indicate the difficulty of maintaining the required diet. A large number of people note frequent breakdowns, as a plus, they talk about a fairly easy retention of reduced weight. On average, 2 kilograms are returned out of 10.

To get out of this diet, you need to gradually add vegetables to dairy products first, then cereals and meat. It should take you 3-4 days to return to your normal diet. The dairy diet has a certain list of contraindications, including intolerance to milk, the presence of allergic reactions reactions to food, problems with the gastrointestinal tract. To protect your teeth, you need to rinse your mouth after taking milk, because there is an accelerated growth of bacteria in the milk environment. Milk helps to remove harmful substances from the body, the smell of sweat can become sharp and specific, so while following the diet, you need to pay more attention to personal hygiene issues.
