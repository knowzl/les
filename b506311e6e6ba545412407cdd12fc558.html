Slim and flat stomach with the help of vacuum. Execution technique.

Vacuum, taken from yoga, is an exercise that bodybuilders, fitness trainers, and many others are willing to use. This is a miracle exercise that makes the stomach flat without doing hours of fitness or doing anything else. twistings'.

The point is to train the farthest abdominal muscle, the transverse one, to make it elastic, and a flat stomach is a gift.

Advantages of a vacuum.

  1. The waist becomes thinner.
  2. Stomach it's pulling itself up.
  3. Vacuum removes harmful substances from the body by massaging the internal organs.
  4. General improvement of the abdominal cavity and small pelvis.
  5. Improves blood flow.
  6. It tones the muscles.
  7. Forms a corset and a beautiful posture.
  8. Stimulates digestion.
  9. Promotes proper functioning of the gastrointestinal tract.
  10. Removes back pain.

Contraindications:

  • Gastritis;
  • Ulcer;
  • Pancreatitis;
  • Colitises;
  • Diseases of the biliary tract.
  • Pregnancy;
  • Menstruation;
  • Full stomach.

Vacuum at home.

There are 4 types of this exercise.

  • Lying on your back;
  • Sitting;
  • On all fours;
  • Standing position.

Consider the execution technique.

Lying on your back.

The easiest way to run it vacuum's.

Lie on your back, arms at your sides, your lower back pressed to the floor, legs bent at the knees.

Inhale through the nose, exhale through the mouth, saying "Paaaaa", take all the air out of the lungs, pull in the stomach, so that the ribs stick out. Stay in this position as long as possible, starting with 10-15 seconds.

Inhale the air and relax the stomach 3-5 repetitions.

Vacuum sitting.

More complex execution and no less effective.

We sit down on a chair or sofa.

Through the nose we draw it in the air, through the rod, we exhale, saying "Paaaaa", we pull in the stomach, so that the ribs stick out.

Hold this position for 10-15 seconds. 3-5 repetitions.

On all fours.

We get up on all fours, knees under the pelvis, palms under the shoulder, the hands are even.

Just as in the past types of vykuuma inhale through the nose and exhale through the mouth, saying "Paaaaa" (this way all the air comes out more effectively), we draw in the stomach as much as possible. We stand for 10-15 seconds or more.

Important!
The vacuum is done only on an empty stomach, otherwise there will be no effect.

Standing position.

This type of exercise is the most difficult.

Standing on your feet, slightly tilt the body forward, legs bent at the knees, hands on your knees.

Inhaled air through the nose and sharply exhale through the mouth, saying "Paaaaa", pull in the stomach, so that the ribs stick out.

We stand in this position as much as possible and repeat 5 times.

The first results can be noticed in 2 days weeks.

A simple exercise that can be done anywhere: at home, at work, in a fitness club, at any time.

I use it on days when there is no training or combine it with the main one. And it is better to perform every day in the morning and a slim figure and a flat stomach are provided for you.
